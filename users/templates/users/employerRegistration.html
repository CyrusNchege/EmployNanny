Terms and Conditions for Nannies
  • Platform Usage:
    • All chats and communication should take place on the platform.
  • Account and Payments:
    • Creating an account is free, but once you post a job, you need to make payments for your job.
  • Payment Verified Jobs:
    • Jobs that have a "payment verified" badge tend to receive more applications.
  • Client Details and Valid IDs:
    • Check the details of clients and ensure their IDs are valid.
  • Contract Terms:
    • While giving a contract, ensure that you have agreed on various aspects such as leave, vacation, and payment terms.
    • You can also include salary increments while renegotiating payment.
  • Multiple Nannies or Jobs:
    • You can have more than one nanny per job or post multiple jobs, but ensure that the payment is above the minimum.
  • Account Balance:
    • Always check your balance, and if it's low, you may receive frequent messages to add more money.
  • Contract Termination and Feedback:
    • In case of contract termination, provide a reason and leave feedback.
  • Platform Communication and Reporting:
    • All conversations and transactions must happen on the platform. In case of any issues, report them immediately to the nearest police (we can always help).
